Energy Saving Tips:

  • Turn off lights when leaving a room
  • Unplug devices when not in use (they still use "phantom power")
  • Use natural light during the day
  • Choose energy-efficient appliances (look for Energy Star label)
  • Set thermostats a few degrees higher in summer and lower in winter
