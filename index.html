How kijanihub uses satellites for biodiversity tracking.

posted by admin march 2018

Kijani Hub leverages satellite imagery and remote sensing technologies to monitor biodiversity changes and habitat conditions in real time. By analyzing satellite data, the platform identifies deforestation patterns, species distribution, and ecosystem health across various regions. This data-driven approach enhances conservation efforts by providing accurate insights for sustainable resource management and policy advocacy.

best way to stop environmental pollution

posted by admin march 2018

The best way to stop environmental pollution using tools like Kijani Hub is by integrating technology and community action. Platforms like Kijani Hub use AI, satellite data, and real-time monitoring to track pollution sources and provide actionable insights for mitigation. Coupled with community education and locally driven solutions, these tools empower stakeholders to adopt sustainable practices and enforce accountability for a cleaner environment.

green education by learnig GIS

posted by admin march 2018

Green education through learning GIS equips individuals with the skills to map and analyze environmental data, supporting sustainable decision-making. GIS technology enables users to monitor land use, track deforestation, and identify areas impacted by climate change or pollution. By integrating GIS into green education, students and professionals gain practical tools to address environmental challenges and implement data-driven conservation strategies.

Axolotl

london, UK

The axolotl is a paedomorphic salamander closely related to the tiger salamander. It is unusual among amphibians in that it reaches adulthood without undergoing metamorphosis. Instead of taking to the land, adults remain aquatic and gilled.

blue Dragon

london, UK

Glaucus atlanticus is a species of sea slug in the family Glaucidae. These sea slugs live in the pelagic zone, where they float upside-down by using the surface tension of the water to stay afloat. They are carried along by the winds and ocean currents.

sting ray

london, UK

Stingrays are a group of sea rays, a type of cartilaginous fish. They are classified in the suborder Myliobatoidei of the order Myliobatiformes and consist of eight families: Hexatrygonidae, Plesiobatidae, Urolophidae, Urotrygonidae, Dasyatidae, Potamotrygonidae, Gymnuridae and Myliobatidae.

Canada lynx

Canada

Characterized by its long, dense fur, snowshoe-like paws, and black-tufted ears, the Canada lynx (Lynx canadensis) is native to Canada and the US states of Alaska, Maine, Montana, Washington, Minnesota, Colorado (and possibly Wyoming, Wisconsin, Michigan, and New Hampshire). They live in boreal forests and mainly target snowshoe hares as prey.
